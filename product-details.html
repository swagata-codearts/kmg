Freemax Maxus 50W Kit

The Freemax Maxus 50W kit is all about compact power, starting with the Maxus 50W variable wattage mod. Featuring a 2000mAh built-in battery, this device packs a punch with multiple output modes, temperature control, and maximum wattage of 50w. Safety features on the device include short circuit protection, overtime protection, overcharge protection, overdischarge protection, high temperature protection, and lower power protection. It also features USB Type-C for fast charging.

Paired with the Maxus 50W mod is the Freemax Fireluke 22 tank, which takes advantage of FM Coiltech 4.0 technology. A child-resistant certified top fill mechanism makes it easy to refill the tank, while keeping it safe from children. With 3.5ml e-liquid capacity, the Fireluke 22 tank is the perfect match to the Maxus 50W mod.

Freemax Maxus 50W Mod Specifications:

  • Output Voltage: 0.5V to 8.0V
  • Output Wattage: 5w to 50w
  • Temperature Control: 100ºC to 315ºC / 200ºF to 600ºF
  • Output Modes: Power/VPC/TC-TI/TC-SS/TC-NI/TCR
  • Resistance Range: 0.1 ohm to 3.0 ohm
  • Battery Capacity: 2000mAh (internal)
  • Charger Specification: 5V/2A

Freemax Fireluke 22 Tank Specifications:

  • Dimensions: 51.41mm by 23mm
  • Capacity: 3.5ml
  • Material: SS904L/Tea Fiber Cotton/Resin
  • Working Wattage: 8w to 50w
  • Thread: 510
  • Drip Tip: 510

Includes:

  • 1x Freemax Maxus 50w Mod
  • 1x Freemax Fireluke 22 Tank Resin Edition
  • 1x Freemax DTL Mesh 0.25-ohm Coil (pre-installed)
  • 1x Freemax DTL Mesh 0.5-ohm Coil
  • 1x Extra Pyrex Tube (3.5ml)
  • 1x Extra Set of O-rings
  • 11x USB Type-C Cable
  • 1x Warning Card
  • 1x Warranty Card
  • 1x User Manual
