Pod mods are small, easy-to-carry vaporizers that can fit inside your pocket. Designed with the needs of beginners in mind, pods are easy to use and a perfect option if you’re looking to get started with vaping.

Because of their small size, pods are also known as pod vapes, pod systems, pocket vapes, mini vapes and a variety of other names.

Unlike larger vapes, pod vapes are extremely compact. Many pod vapes are equal in size to a small USB stick, making them easy to store inside your pocket or keep inside your backpack, handbag or satchel while you’re out and about.

Pods have a simple design that makes them easy to use and reliable. Most pod vaporizers use a two-party system that’s made up of an e-liquid pod and a battery. The battery makes up most of the physical size of the pod mod, with the pod itself clicking into the battery for use.

While most pod systems have refillable pods, other are sold pre-filled with an e-liquid flavor. Like other vaporizers, most pod mods can be charged via USB, making it easy to keep a pod mod topped up with power as you go about your day.

Using a pod mod is very simple. Some pods have a power button that you can push down to activate the vaporizer. Others are automatic and will quickly start producing vapor as soon as you take a drag.

Because of their simplicity, compact size and convenience, pod mods are very popular. They’re commonly used by smokers aiming to transition from smoking into vaping, especially those with a long-term goal of quitting smoking altogether.

Compared to other vaporizer types, pods have a few small inconveniences. Since they’re smaller in size than other portable vapes, they tend to have smaller batteries. This means that your pod mod might run out of battery before a larger vape, limiting your usage options.

While most pod vaporizers are extremely affordable, there are some higher-end options that cost slightly more than comparable vapes.In general, pod mods prioritize simplicity and convenience over advanced features, meaning they’re better for casual vapers and first-timers than serious vape enthusiasts.

If you’re a smoker looking to transition to vaping for health reasons, or as part of a long-term effort to quit smoking, you’ll probably find a lot to like about pod vapes. Using a pod mod isn’t very different from smoking a cigarette, meaning there’s little to no learning curve.

Pod systems also draw somewhat like a cigarette, making it easier to change your habits from smoking to vaping as a former cigarette smoker.

The small size of cheap vape mods also makes them a great choice for cigarette smokers. Most pods are compact enough to fit inside a pocket and have a similar profile to a cigarette lighter, making it easy to switch out your lighter for a convenient, similarly-shaped pod vape.

While pod mods are most popular with beginners and smokers hoping to transition to vaping, they’re also widely used by vaping enthusiasts. The tiny size of the average pod mod makes this type of vaporizer perfect as a second, “to-go” vape for enthusiasts and serious vapers.

Because pod vaporizers are also affordable, they’re easy to add to your vaping collection without spending more than you’d like to.

Beyond their compact size and affordability, pods are extremely easy to use. If you prefer fewer features to too many, you’ll enjoy the simple design and easy operation a pod mod can offer. After all, more features don’t necessarily produce a better vaporizer.

Finally, cheap vape mods are great choices for vapers who’d like to enjoy vaping while on the go or involved in social activities without the obviousness of a larger vape setup. Using a pod mod can be a discreet activity, making this type of vaporizer great for social situations. Most pod mods come in two different designs: pre-filled systems and refillable systems. Each type of pod vaporizer offers its own range of advantages and disadvantages, from convenience to extra versatility in terms of flavor.

Pre-filled pod systems use pre-filled pods, or cartridges. These pods come filled with a specific type of e-liquid. To use them, all you need to do is attach the e-liquid pod to the vape — there’s no need to manually fill the pod yourself.

The key advantage of a pre-filled pod system is convenience. Since you don’t need to fill up the pod with e-liquid by yourself, you can enjoy your favorite vape flavors with a design that allows for “plug and play” vaping.

The biggest disadvantage of a pre-filled pod system is the lack of versatility. Since you can only use pre-filled pods with a pre-filled pod mod (or “closed system” vape), some e-liquid flavors you might want to try may not be compatible with your pod mod.

Refillable pod systems use an empty, refillable pod that you can fill yourself. This means you’re free to choose any type of e-liquid flavor and aren’t limited to flavors that are sold in the form of pre-filled cartridges.

Obviously, the biggest advantage of a refillable pod system is that you have total control over the flavors you choose.

The key disadvantage of a refillable pod system, on the other hand, is the lower convenience level. Since you’ll need to fill each pod with e-liquid yourself, it can take a little bit longer to set up your pod mod and start vaping, compared to a faster, easier pre-filled system.

Ultimately, there’s no “best” type of pod mod. If you prefer convenience to extra flavor options, you’ll likely enjoy the benefits of a pre-filled pod system. If you prefer to be able to choose from a wider range of e-liquid flavors, you’ll probably prefer a refillable pod system.Because of their compact design, pod mods generally don’t produce as much vapor as larger, more powerful vaping systems.

This means they’ll deliver slightly less nicotine in vapor than other systems. Because of this, many people choose to use e-liquids made with nicotine salts with their pod vapes, as these e-liquids tend to have higher concentrations of nicotine.

In addition to e-liquids made from nicotine salts, it’s generally okay to use any e-liquid with a propylene glycol (PG) content of 50% or more. Ultimately, the best choice of e-liquid will vary depending on your specific pod vape.
