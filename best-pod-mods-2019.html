Best Pod Mods To Buy in 2019

Pod mods and devices are some of the simplest and easiest-to-use vape kits you can find. Typically, these products include empty pods that you can fill yourself with your preferred e-liquid, or come with a closed pod that already contains vape juice. Most people choose their pods depending on ease of use, and recently pods mods have become more popular since they’re so convenient and compact. Here is a list of the our best pod mods of 2019.

iPV Aspect

While making the Aspect, iPV didn’t leave anything to chance. This compact, pocket-friendly, and easy-to-use device is our pick for top pod mod of 2019. Featuring a built-in 750mAh battery, the iPV Aspect is designed to last you all day long and more, meaning you won’t constantly have to be charging your device.

There is even airflow adjustability on the pod for further customization of your vaping experience.

Suorin Edge

Suorin is one of the most well known and reputable manufacturers when it comes to pod mods and the Edge might be one of the company’s best designs yet. The ultra compact design is fits easily in your pocket. Following up on the popular Air, Drop, and Vagon, the Edge may only have a battery capacity of 230mAh, but they’re interchangeable! That means you can always keep one battery fully charged for easy swapping for an all-day vaping experience.The Suorin Edge also takes advantage of USB-C, which means it can be fully charged in as little as 30 minutes. With a variety of color options, you simply can’t go wrong with the Suorin Edge if you like its design.

Like other Suorin products, the Edge uses draw-activated firing and has an LED battery life indicator. Constructed from zinc alloy, it measures 49mm by 12mm by 73mm and has a safety lock button so you can store it away without worrying about misfiring. Each Suorin Edge pod features 1.5ml capacity.

Pros

  • Swappable/interchangeable batteries
  • Sleek design
  • Draw-activated firing
  • LED battery life indicator
  • USB charging
  • Zinc alloy construction
  • Safety lock button

SMOK Nord

When it comes to vaping products, not very many brands has the same notoriety as SMOK. The Nord Kit is a fantastic and ultra compact pod device, using a button for activation. What makes it extremely impressive is the built-in 1100mAh battery, which results in a very long-lasting vaping experience before you have to plug it in.Users also love the fact that the SMOK Nord is compatible with two different coils: 0.6 ohm Mesh and 1.4 ohm Regular. The 0.6 ohm Mesh coil is perfect for sub-ohm vaping, while the 1.4 ohm Regular coil is designed for an excellent MTL experience.

Each pod has 3ml e-liquid capacity, which works perfectly with the large internal battery. This means you can vape all day long without having to recharge or refill.

Safety features on the SMOK Nord include lithium battery protection, 8 seconds cut-off, short circuit protection, and low voltage protection. Output wattage on this device ranges from 10w to 15w while its overall dimensions are 94mm by 30mm by 18.8mm. It tips the scales at just 80 grams.

Pros

  • Ultra compact and lightweight
  • 1100mAh built-in battery
  • 3ml pod capacity
  • 500mAh internal battery
  • Compatible with two different coils (0.6 Mesh and 1.4 Regular)
  • Plenty of safety features

Cons

  • Honeycomb design isn’t for everybody
  • Removing the pod isn’t always easy

SXmini Mi Class

If you’re looking for a high-quality pod mod that will impress all your friends, take a closer look at the SXmini Mi Class. For years, SXmini has designed and manufactured some of the highest quality devices the vaping industry has ever seen, and the Mi Class is no different.This stylish and slim pod mod is different than what you’re used to seeing, manufactured from stainless steel with a photochemical etching process.

The Mi Class uses 1.8ml pods that are easy to refill and remove, powered by a ceramic coil with 1.0 ohm resistance. There is also an LED display on the device, which shows battery life, voltage, and has a puff counter.

At the bottom of the Mi Class is a USB port for charging the 400mAh built-in battery. This device measures 20.6mm by 85.5mm by 20.2mm and has a power range of 7w to 13w.
